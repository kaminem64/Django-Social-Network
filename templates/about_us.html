About Solutioner

Solutioner is a free Solution Sharing Social Network.

We provide a wide variety of social services as follows

  • Search for solutions
  • Find whether a solution is useful or not by color specified for solution based on your/others vote(s)
  • Making/Editing profiles and providing information to others
  • Managing permissions based on newest security standards
  • Adding new solutions
  • Collecting others useful solutions
  • Forking others useful solutions with respect to permissions
  • -100 to +100 based voting system
  • Making solution books out of your ot others solutions with respect to permissions
  • Following others activities by liking them
